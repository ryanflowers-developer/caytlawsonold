A shootout leaves Kit Stone and her brothers running from the law. Amidst this turmoil, another responsibility falls heavy on Kit’s shoulders. But with her family torn apart and scattered to the wind, it was a duty she’d have to face alone; a prospect that would perhaps frighten a lesser woman, but not Kit Stone.

Benjamin Price, an unwanted second son of an English Earl, may have been born to a life of gentility, but he was no dandy - despite Kit Stone’s accusations. And he wasn’t about to let her set off on her own; she needed him.

And it was time she knew it.
