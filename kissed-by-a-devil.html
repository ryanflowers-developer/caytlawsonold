Lavinia Stone had sworn off men for good. In fact, she never even wanted to be in close proximity to one again. So how did she find herself in the role of nursemaid tending to the contrary, Gabriel Marks? The man had been a thorn in her side for years. And now he was the most dangerous man of her acquaintance; for he knew her secrets…

Gabriel Marks never thought to have the ranch or the girl of his dreams, but when both fall into his hands, he knows he must find a way to hold onto them.

The problem was, he no longer felt worthy of either…

When two broken souls come together, they become whole.
