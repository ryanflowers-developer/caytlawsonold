Cayt Lawson’s debut into Historical Romance begins with Ann’s Valley – the prequel to her upcoming Stone Ranch Series…

In Ann’s Valley, Regency meets Western as readers follow an English debutante’s journey from the ballrooms of London to America’s wild frontier. She is on a mission to restore herself to the life she led before, and she’ll allow no one to stand in her way; especially not an obstinate mountain man underestimating her at every turn.
